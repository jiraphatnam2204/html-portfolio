The Best Movies According to James

My top 3 movies of all time.

Carry On

It is about a TSA officer working at Los Angeles International Airport who is blackmailed by a traveler.

Geostorm

It is a science-fiction disaster film about a satellite designer who tries to save the world from the malfunctioning of climate-controlling satellites.

Jujutsu Kaisen 0: The Movie

It serves as a prologue to the manga series Jujutsu Kaisen.
